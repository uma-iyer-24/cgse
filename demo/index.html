The challenge

Most methods need a large teacher. We don't.

Knowledge Distillation borrows accuracy from a pretrained teacher — but that teacher costs extra compute on every single training batch. CGSE replaces it with a self-trained critic.

Traditional KD

Teacher + Student pipeline

A ResNet-56 teacher must run a forward pass on every training batch. Over 50 epochs that's 19,550 extra forward passes — unavoidable overhead at every step.

ResNet-56 Teacher → ResNet-20 Student → 91.66% acc

CGSE (ours)

Critic-guided self-evolution

A tiny 3-layer critic observes optimization statistics each epoch to decide when and how to structurally mutate the student — zero teacher overhead.

DiscreteCritic → ResNet-20 Student → 90.85% acc

Methodology

Train → Observe → Mutate → Continue

Four stages that let the network discover its own optimal architecture during training — guided entirely by what the critic learns from optimization signals.

1
Warm-up
Student trains normally with SGD + cosine LR
2
Critic observes
Reads 8-dim state: loss, acc, Δacc, params…
3
Decide
Critic picks: noop / head-widen / layer3-widen / deepen
4
Resume & improve
Function-preserving init; training continues seamlessly

Competitive advantage

Where CGSE beats existing systems

CGSE doesn't always top raw accuracy — but it wins where deployment actually matters.

🚫

Teacher-free evolution

CGSE achieves structural evolution with zero teacher forward passes. Traditional KD must run a full 855K-param ResNet-56 on every training batch.

0 teacher fwd (vs 19,550)
⚡

Faster wall-clock training

By eliminating teacher inference entirely, CGSE finishes 50-epoch training in 2,846s versus Student KD's 3,045s — 6.5% faster end-to-end.

2,846s vs 3,045s (−6.5%)
🧠

Learns when to grow

The critic is trained with REINFORCE to maximise post-mutation validation gain. It learns early mutations hurt, late-stage widening helps.

REINFORCE · 8-dim state
Real-world impact

Where teacher-free evolution matters

Any domain where a large pretrained teacher doesn't exist or can't run alongside the student benefits directly from CGSE.

📱

Edge & mobile

Train compact models on-device without cloud teacher inference — critical for privacy-sensitive and offline deployments.

🏥

Medical imaging

Rare-disease datasets rarely have large pretrained teachers. CGSE finds efficient architectures from scratch with no external dependency.

🚗

Autonomous vehicles

On-device model adaptation under strict compute budgets — grow only the layers that matter for the current driving scenario.

🛰️

Satellite & drone

Low-power inference pipelines that self-tune architecture based on available compute — no external teacher required mid-flight.
